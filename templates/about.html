Abouts Bagrote Valley

Bagrote Valley,located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of northern Pakistan ,is a hidden gem know for its stunning landscape ,rich culture and serene environment.Nestled between towering mountains and lush green field , this valley offers an extraordinary view if snow-capped peaks especially the Rakaposhi,Diran,Bilchhar Dobani and other surrounding mountains

features of Bagrote

one of the most distinctive features of bagrote is its agricultural heritage.The valley is known for terraced fields and orchards where locals grow apple,apricots,walnuts,cherries,etc

History of Bagrote

The people of Bagrote are predominantly of the Burusho and shina ethnic groups they speak the pure shina language.They are known for their warm hospitality,traditional lifestyle and unique culture that has been preserved for centuries.Visitors often find a welcoming community thst is eager to share its history,folklore,and traditional customs
